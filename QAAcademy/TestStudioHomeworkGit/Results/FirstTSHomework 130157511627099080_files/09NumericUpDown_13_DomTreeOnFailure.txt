<DIV style="BORDER-BOTTOM: 0px; BORDER-LEFT: 0px; PADDING-BOTTOM: 0px; OVERFLOW-Y: hidden; MARGIN: 0px; PADDING-LEFT: 0px; PADDING-RIGHT: 0px; HEIGHT: auto; VISIBILITY: visible; OVERFLOW: hidden; BORDER-TOP: 0px; BORDER-RIGHT: 0px; PADDING-TOP: 0px">
<DIV style="HEIGHT: auto; VISIBILITY: visible; OVERFLOW: hidden" id=ctl00_SampleContent_Description_ContentPanel>
<P>NumericUpDown is an ASP.NET AJAX extender that can be attached to an ASP.NET TextBox control to add "up" and "down" buttons that increment and decrement the value in the TextBox. The increment and decrement can be simple +1/-1 arithmetic, they can cycle through a provided list of values (like the months of the year), or they can call a Web Service to determine the next value. <LI><STRONG>Tag</STRONG> - Specifies a custom parameter to pass to the Web Service</LI>
<LI><STRONG>Minimum</STRONG> - The minimum value allowed by the extender. Currently, it does not prevent out of range values from being entered into the textbox even if Minimum or Maximum are specified on the extender, but using the up/down buttons should bring the value into the allowed range when clicked.</LI>
<LI><STRONG>Maximum</STRONG> - The maximum value allowed by the extender.</LI></UL></DIV></DIV>
